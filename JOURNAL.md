In fusion I started by create a new assembly and then a new sketch. This tells Fusion that the part I am now making is not a component of the screen, but independent in its own way.

![image](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1773571733179-yh4wch.png)

Instead of creating each component in the controller board, which is time consuming, and not required, I decided to make the controller board a cuboid. With respect to the switch panel of the controller board, since I will not be placing it within the exclosure I will create a small gap next to the controller board for wiring and stick it onto the exclosure for the monitor.
